She taps him on the shoulder with a paper-knife and says huskily: "Rise, Sir Harry."

He rises, bows, and glides about the room, going on his knees to various articles of furniture, and rises from each a knight.

It is a radiant domestic scene, and_ HARRY _is as dignified as if he knew that royalty was rehearsing it at the other end_.
